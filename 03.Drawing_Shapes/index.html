Drawing Shapes

The next port of call is drawing shapes

With D3's powerful DOM manipulation methods, we can easily and seamlessly create shapes used to represent data, by the manipulation of SVG elements.

Since SVG elements are just like regular HTML DOM nodes, but having special attributes for manipulating their shapes, leveraging D3's functionality enables us to manipulate their shape with attributes like radius, co-ordinates, height, color, etc. in other to represent data quantitatively.
